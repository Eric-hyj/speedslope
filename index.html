How to Play Speed Slope

Controls

Use A/D keys or Left/Right Arrow keys to move the ball left and right. On mobile, use touch controls to steer.

Objective

Survive as long as possible without falling off the edge or hitting obstacles. The further you go, the higher your score.

Pro Tips

Don't stare at the ball — look ahead at the track ahead. Stay calm at high speeds; overcorrecting will send you flying off. Land properly on slopes — a good landing saves a run, a bad one ends it.

Speed Slope Tips & Tricks

  • Look ahead at the track, not at the ball — this helps you anticipate turns
  • Don't panic at high speeds — overcorrecting is worse than slight adjustments
  • Landing spots on ramps can make or break your run — aim for the center
  • If you feel like you're losing control, slow down your reactions slightly — sometimes less is more
  • The hardest part isn't the obstacles — it's the panic that sets in when the speed gets extreme
  • After a crash, restart immediately — that's how you build muscle memory
